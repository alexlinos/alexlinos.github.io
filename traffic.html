Support Services / Traffic Division

The responsibilities of the Support Services / Traffic Division include, but are not limited to:

  • Accident investigations
  • Funeral escorts
  • Speed enforcement and monitoring
  • Purchasing, upfitting and maintenance of all PD fleet vehicles
  • Maintenance of the PD building and property
  • Coordinating of special events throughout the town
  • Hiring, training & supervising of all school crossing guards
  • Participating with area departments for regional enforcement events
  • Installing, repairing & replacing all town-owned street/traffic signs & traffic lights
  • Securing state grants for specialized enforcement(cell phone, DUI, seatbelts, etc.)
